How To Buy Bitcoin

Follow these steps and say hello to your first crypto purchase

by Dan Haas, May 10 2021

If you're reading this, you're filled with curiosity about cryptocurrency and want to buy some for yourself. Perhaps you've heard about it from family or friends or the news. Cryptocurrency is becoming more and more popular, and it's natural to want to try this new currency for yourself. Don't worry, it can be easy to buy cryptocurrency. Some are easier than others - the easiest by far is Bitcoin. Here you can discover how to get some for yourself.

There are many ways to buy bitcoin. Here are three:

  1. Buy with PayPal
  2. Buy with an app like Coinbase
  3. Buy at select ATMs

Let's go one by one and see how easy it is to get each one.

1. Buy With PayPal

Notice: the author works for PayPal, but receives no incentive for this article.

To buy with PayPal, open the PayPal app on your smartphone. Create an account if you don't have one already. Here, you may be prompted to complete a form similar to what you might see at a Bank since you will be making monetary transactions. This information is secure with PayPal; you probably shared most of this information with them to use their services before. Note: There are many untrustworthy services which sell crypto, so before trying to buy on other platforms which ask for your personal information, do your research! Find out as much as you can about the security of those platforms before you share your financial or personally identifying data. PayPal is widely considered secure.

Click the 'Crypto' button on the bottom of the screen. You will see a list of cryptocurrencies and Bitcoin is at the top - click Bitcoin. Next you'll see a screen with a graph showing how much Bitcoin costs right now. It's a very expensive coin: over $56,000 as of May 10 2021, but don't worry, you can spend whatever amount you like and buy just a fraction of a Bitcoin. Then click the Buy button. Here you will be prompted to read a statement so you as a customer have been informed of the nature of cryptocurrency. Read it, and if you agree, hit the agree button.

Next, choose the amount of money you want to spend on Bitcoin and fill that in the amount field. There is a transaction fee of $0.50 and a fee of some size is to be expected for any cryptocurrency purchases. Click Buy.

You should now see some confetti on your screen. Congratulations, you have bought your first Bitcoin!
