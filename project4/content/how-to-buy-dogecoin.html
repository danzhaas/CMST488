How To Buy Dogecoin

This popular crypto is tricky, but possible to buy for yourself

by Dan Haas, May 11 2021

How to buy Dogecoin:

  1. Create Coinbase account
  2. Buy Bitcoin
  3. Wait about 7 days for Bitcoin to become available to send
  4. Create Binance account
  5. Send Bitcoin to Binance Account
  6. Sell Bitcoin for USD on Binance
  7. Buy Dogecoin with USD on Binance
  8. Set up Coinbase Wallet
  9. Send Dogecoin to Coinbase Wallet for secure storage
  10. Hold On for Dear Life (HODL) until you're ready to sell
